# Privacy Policy for Petmoni

**Last Updated: December 25, 2025**

Petmoni ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their beloved pets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide you with high-quality pet health tracking and AI insights, we collect:
* **Account Information:** Email address or third-party login data (e.g., Apple ID).
* **Pet Profiles:** Name, breed, age, weight, and health records.
* **Media Data:** Photos and videos of your pets that you choose to upload for "AI Vision Recognition."
* **Health & Activity Data:** Diet records, bowel movement logs, and vaccination history.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
* Generate **Weekly Health Intelligence** reports using AI.
* Provide **AI Vet Consultations** based on your logs.
* Sync your data across devices via secure cloud services.
* Process your Pro/Ultra subscriptions.

## 3. AI Data Processing (DeepSeek/GPT)
Petmoni utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) to analyze pet health data. 
* **Data Transmission:** When you request an AI report or consultation, relevant pet data is sent to our AI partners (e.g., DeepSeek, OpenAI) via secure APIs.
* **Anonymization:** We strip personally identifiable information (like your name or email) before sending pet health logs to AI engines.
* **No Training:** We ensure through our API agreements that your data is **not** used to train third-party AI models.
